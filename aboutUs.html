About Us

PURPOSE: This project was put together at the University of Washington Women in Informatics 6th Annual Hackathon.
We were prompted with the question "What does equality mean to you?"


IMPACT: Millions of people around the world are unable to communicate with a computer via mouse control.
Instead, they use screen reader applications to allow them to operate every aspect of a computer, including surfing the web.
However, websites must have ARIA feature tags in order for screen readers to work properly, which many do not currently have.
The ARIA feature generator helps developers overcome their lack of understanding of how to use ARIA tags, letting their websites
reach an even greater audience than ever before.
